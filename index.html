Harvest Registry

The Harvest Registry is the entry point into the Catalog. IOOS data providers can use the Registry to submit ISO 19115-compliant metadata describing their datasets and associated services using the Registry. The IOOS Catalog requires Web Accessible Folders containing ISO 19115 metadata be published and accessible for the Registry to harvest. More information found here.
